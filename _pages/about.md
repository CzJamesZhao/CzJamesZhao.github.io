I am currently a second-year Master's student at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), advised by Prof. Wendong Wang. Prior to this, I earned my B.E. degree from BUPT with First-Class Honours (Top 5%) and Beijing Outstanding Graduate.

My research is motivated by a deep interest in the modeling of complex visual dynamics. I have a strong foundation in spatiotemporal representation learning and generative modeling, highlighted by my first-author work on efficient dense point tracking (AAAI 2026) and my recent explorations in 3D human generation using diffusion models. 

Currently, I am actively seeking Ph.D. opportunities commencing in Fall 2027. I am deeply motivated to bridge the gap between fine-grained visual comprehension and large-scale synthesis. By leveraging my background in temporal dynamics and foundation models, I aim to push the frontiers of AIGC and Multimodal Large Models, contributing to an expansive framework where machines can seamlessly perceive, reason, and create across varied modalities.
